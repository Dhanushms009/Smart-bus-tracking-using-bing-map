About Bus Tracking

Bus tracking is a technology that allows real-time monitoring and tracking of buses in a transportation system. It utilizes GPS (Global Positioning System) technology to track the exact location of buses on a map in real-time. Bus tracking systems provide passengers with valuable information such as bus locations, estimated arrival times, and any delays or disruptions, enhancing the overall passenger experience and making public transportation more efficient and reliable. These systems also benefit bus operators by optimizing bus routes, improving fleet management, and enhancing safety and security through real-time monitoring.
